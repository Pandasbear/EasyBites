  2. 2

    In another bowl, beat eggs and then whisk in milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract.

  3. 3

    Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Don't overmix - a few lumps are okay!

  4. 4

    Heat a lightly oiled griddle or non-stick pan over medium heat.

  5. 5

    Pour ¬º cup of batter for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form on surface and edges look set, about 2-3 minutes.
